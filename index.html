About the App

Surface Duo Extensions is a utility for the rare Microsoft Surface Duo 2 smartphone. It adds a missing feature: displaying battery level on the Glance Bar.

You no longer need to unfold the device to check battery status — making everyday use more convenient and efficient.

Main Feature

  • 🔋 Shows battery level on the Glance Bar
